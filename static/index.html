About Us

Machine Intelligence
Solving the Rubik’s cube with deep reinforcement learning and A star algorithm.

Our team started the research on intellective games and their solutions by deep learning and search algorithms from September, 2019. Solving the Rubik’s cube in this way is one of our tasks. Hence, the DeepCube project came into being.

Goal

Our team aims to regain the experimental results concerning solutions of the 3x3x3 Rubik's cube on the basis of a paper published on journal of Nature by Forest Agostinelli et al. At the same time, it is also a good opportunity for us to help many children and others keen on knowing this game to learn how to solve the Rubik's cube more effectively.

Philosophy

Our team has been carrying out all kinds of activities with the principle of passion, innovation and contribution.
